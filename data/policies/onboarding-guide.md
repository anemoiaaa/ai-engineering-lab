# New Employee Onboarding Guide

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All new hires (full-time, part-time, contractors)

## 1. Purpose

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the onboarding process at Northstar Foods. It ensures every new employee has a smooth, structured, and welcoming start.

## 2. Before Your First Day (Pre-boarding)

- [ ] Sign and return your **employment contract** via DocuSign.
- [ ] Complete the **background check** authorization form.
- [ ] Submit your **tax forms** (W-4, state withholding) through the HR Portal.
- [ ] Provide **banking details** for direct deposit setup.
- [ ] Review and sign the **NDA** (Non-Disclosure Agreement).
- [ ] Complete the **emergency contact** form.
- [ ] Upload a **professional photo** for your employee badge and directory.

## 3. First Day Checklist

- [ ] Arrive at the office by **9:00 AM** or log in remotely by **9:00 AM** local time.
- [ ] Meet your **onboarding buddy** — assigned to help you during your first 90 days.
- [ ] Collect your **company-issued laptop** and equipment from IT.
- [ ] Set up your **email account** and verify access.
- [ ] Activate **multi-factor authentication (MFA)** on all accounts.
- [ ] Join mandatory Slack channels: **#general**, **#announcements**, **#your-team**.
- [ ] Complete the **IT Security Orientation** (30-minute online module).
- [ ] Review the **Code of Conduct** and acknowledge receipt.

## 4. First Week Tasks

- [ ] Attend the **Welcome Session** with HR (scheduled on your first Monday).
- [ ] Complete all **mandatory compliance training** modules:
  - Anti-harassment training (45 minutes)
  - Data privacy training (30 minutes)
  - Workplace safety training (20 minutes)
- [ ] Schedule **1:1 meetings** with your manager (weekly recurring).
- [ ] Review team **goals and current projects** with your manager.
- [ ] Set up **development environment** (for engineering roles) or **tools and access** (for other roles).
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with the **company intranet** and key resources.
- [ ] Complete your **employee profile** on the HR Portal.

## 5. First Month Goals

- [ ] Complete the **Onboarding Learning Path** on the company's LMS (Learning Management System).
- [ ] Schedule introductory meetings with **key stakeholders** in your department.
- [ ] Draft your **30-60-90 day plan** with your manager.
- [ ] Attend at least one **company social event** or team lunch.
- [ ] Request access to any **additional tools or systems** needed for your role.
- [ ] Review and understand the **expense reimbursement policy**.
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with the **remote work policy** (if applicable).

## 6. First 90 Days (Probationary Period)

- [ ] Complete all **required training** modules.
- [ ] Participate in **weekly 1:1s** with your manager.
- [ ] Receive your **30-day check-in** feedback from your manager.
- [ ] Receive your **60-day check-in** feedback from your manager.
- [ ] Complete the **90-day performance review** with your manager.
- [ ] Provide feedback on the **onboarding experience** via the HR survey.
