# Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026
**Applies To:** All full-time and part-time employees

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for employees who work remotely, whether on a full-time, part-time, or occasional basis. Northstar Foods is committed to providing flexible work arrangements while maintaining productivity, collaboration, and security standards.

## 2. Eligibility

- All employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible to apply for remote work.
- Remote work eligibility is determined by the employee's manager and HR based on role requirements.
- Roles that require physical presence (e.g., facilities, on-site operations) may not be eligible.

## 3. Remote Work Arrangements

### 3.1 Fully Remote
- Employees work from home or an approved remote location 5 days per week.
- Must be available during core hours: **10:00 AM – 4:00 PM** (local time zone).
- Required to attend in-person meetings or events with at least 2 weeks' notice.

### 3.2 Hybrid
- Employees work remotely 2–3 days per week and in-office the remaining days.
- In-office days are determined by the team and manager.
- Default in-office days are **Tuesday and Thursday** unless otherwise agreed.

### 3.3 Occasional Remote
- Employees may request up to **5 remote work days per month** without a formal arrangement.
- Must be approved by the direct manager at least 24 hours in advance.

## 4. Workspace Requirements

- Employees must have a dedicated, quiet workspace with a reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download).
- Northstar Foods will provide a one-time **$500 stipend** for home office setup for employees on fully remote or hybrid arrangements.
- Employees are responsible for maintaining their home office equipment.

## 5. Communication and Availability

- Employees must be reachable via Slack and email during core hours.
- Video must be enabled during team meetings unless bandwidth issues prevent it.
- Response time expectation: **30 minutes** during core hours for urgent messages.

## 6. Security

- All work must be conducted on company-issued devices or approved BYOD devices.
- VPN must be active when accessing company systems from remote locations.
- Employees must not work from public Wi-Fi networks without VPN protection.
- Sensitive documents must not be printed at home without manager approval.

## 7. Performance and Accountability

- Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees.
- Managers will conduct monthly check-ins to review remote work effectiveness.
- Remote work privileges may be revoked if performance standards are not met.
