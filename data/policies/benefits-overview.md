# Employee Benefits Overview

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026
**Applies To:** All full-time employees (part-time employees may qualify for select benefits)

## 1. Purpose

This document provides an overview of the benefits package offered by Northstar Foods. Our benefits are designed to support employee well-being, financial security, and professional growth.

## 2. Health and Wellness

### 2.1 Medical Insurance
- Northstar Foods covers **80%** of premiums for employee-only plans.
- Northstar Foods covers **60%** of premiums for family plans.
- Three plan options: **Basic**, **Standard**, and **Premium** (details in the Benefits Portal).
- Coverage begins on the **first day of employment**.
- Open enrollment period: **November 1–15** each year.

### 2.2 Dental Insurance
- Fully covered for preventive care (cleanings, X-rays).
- 80% coverage for basic procedures; 50% for major procedures.
- Annual maximum benefit: **$2,000** per person.

### 2.3 Vision Insurance
- Annual eye exam: fully covered.
- Frames/lenses allowance: **$200/year**.
- Contact lenses allowance: **$150/year**.

### 2.4 Mental Health
- **8 free counseling sessions** per year through the Employee Assistance Program (EAP).
- Access to the **Calm** app for meditation and stress management (company-sponsored).
- Mental health days count as sick leave.

## 3. Financial Benefits

### 3.1 Retirement Plan (401k)
- Company matches **100% of the first 4%** and **50% of the next 2%** of employee contributions.
- Vesting schedule: **immediate vesting** for employee contributions; **3-year cliff vesting** for company match.
- Employees can enroll from their first day.

### 3.2 Life Insurance
- Basic life insurance: **2x annual salary** (company-paid).
- Supplemental life insurance available at employee's expense.

### 3.3 Disability Insurance
- Short-term disability: **60% of salary** for up to 26 weeks.
- Long-term disability: **60% of salary** after 26 weeks (company-paid).

### 3.4 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)
- Eligible after **1 year** of employment.
- Purchase company stock at a **15% discount** from market price.
- Maximum contribution: **10% of base salary**.

## 4. Work-Life Balance

- **Flexible Hours:** Core hours are 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM; start/end times are flexible.
- **Remote Work:** Available per the Remote Work Policy.
- **Paid Time Off:** Per the Leave and Time-Off Policy.
- **Sabbatical:** After 7 years of service, employees are eligible for a **4-week paid sabbatical**.

## 5. Professional Development

- **Annual learning budget:** $2,000 per employee for courses, conferences, and certifications.
- **Tuition reimbursement:** Up to **$5,250/year** for degree programs relevant to the employee's role.
- **Internal mentorship program:** Quarterly matching with senior leaders.
- **Book allowance:** $50/quarter for professional development books.

## 6. Additional Perks

- **Commuter benefits:** Pre-tax transit and parking benefits up to IRS limits.
- **Gym membership:** 50% reimbursement up to $75/month.
- **Company events:** Quarterly team outings, annual company retreat, holiday party.
- **Referral bonus:** $3,000 for successful employee referrals (paid after 90 days of referred employee's tenure).
- **Pet-friendly office:** Pets are welcome on Fridays in designated areas.

## 7. Enrollment and Changes

- Benefits enrollment is completed during **onboarding** and **annual open enrollment** (November 1–15).
- Life events (marriage, birth, adoption) allow **mid-year changes** within 30 days of the event.
- All benefits are managed through the **Benefits Portal** at benefits.companyx.com.
