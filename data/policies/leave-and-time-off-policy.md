# Leave and Time-Off Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All full-time employees (part-time employees receive prorated benefits)

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the types of leave available to employees, the process for requesting time off, and the company's expectations regarding attendance and availability.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO)

### 2.1 Annual Allocation
| Tenure | PTO Days per Year |
|--------|-------------------|
| 0–2 years | 15 days |
| 3–5 years | 20 days |
| 6–10 years | 25 days |
| 10+ years | 30 days |

### 2.2 Carryover
- Employees may carry over a maximum of **5 unused PTO days** to the following year.
- Carried-over days must be used by **March 31** of the following year.
- Unused days beyond the carryover limit are forfeited.

### 2.3 Request Process
- PTO requests must be submitted via the **HR Portal** at least **5 business days** in advance.
- Requests of 5 or more consecutive days require **10 business days** advance notice.
- Approval is at the discretion of the employee's direct manager.

## 3. Sick Leave

- Employees receive **10 sick days** per year.
- Sick leave does not carry over between years.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**.
- Employees must notify their manager by **9:00 AM** on the day of absence.

## 4. Parental Leave

- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks of paid leave at 100% salary.
- **Secondary caregiver:** 4 weeks of paid leave at 100% salary.
- Parental leave must be taken within **12 months** of the birth or adoption.
- Employees must notify HR at least **8 weeks** before the expected leave start date.

## 5. Bereavement Leave

- **Immediate family** (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 paid days.
- **Extended family** (grandparent, aunt, uncle, in-law): 3 paid days.
- Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager's discretion.

## 6. Public Holidays

Northstar Foods observes **10 public holidays** per year. The holiday calendar is published in the HR Portal by December 1 of the preceding year.

## 7. Unpaid Leave

- Employees may request unpaid leave for personal reasons.
- Requests must be submitted at least **20 business days** in advance.
- Unpaid leave exceeding 30 days may affect benefits eligibility.
