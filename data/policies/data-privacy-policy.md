# Data Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Legal and Compliance Department
**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026
**Applies To:** All employees, contractors, and third parties who handle personal data

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how Northstar Foods collects, processes, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with applicable privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, and local data protection laws).

## 2. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Any information that can identify a natural person (name, email, phone, IP address, etc.).
- **Sensitive Personal Data:** Health records, biometric data, racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs.
- **Data Subject:** The individual whose personal data is processed.
- **Data Controller:** Northstar Foods, which determines the purposes of data processing.
- **Data Processor:** Any third party that processes data on behalf of Northstar Foods.

## 3. Data Collection Principles

- **Lawfulness:** Data collection must have a legal basis (consent, contract, legitimate interest).
- **Purpose Limitation:** Data is collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
- **Data Minimization:** Only necessary data is collected — no more than what is required.
- **Accuracy:** Data must be kept accurate and up to date.
- **Storage Limitation:** Data is retained only as long as necessary for its purpose.

## 4. Employee Responsibilities

- Employees must only access personal data that is necessary for their job functions.
- Personal data must not be shared with unauthorized parties, internally or externally.
- Employees must report suspected data breaches to the **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** within **1 hour**.
- Training on data privacy is mandatory annually.

## 5. Data Subject Rights

Northstar Foods respects the following rights of data subjects:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Right to request a copy of their personal data |
| **Rectification** | Right to correct inaccurate data |
| **Erasure** | Right to request deletion of their data ("right to be forgotten") |
| **Portability** | Right to receive their data in a structured, machine-readable format |
| **Objection** | Right to object to data processing |
| **Restriction** | Right to request limited processing of their data |

- Requests must be fulfilled within **30 days**.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Employee records | Duration of employment + 7 years |
| Candidate applications | 2 years from application date |
| Client contracts | Duration of contract + 10 years |
| Financial records | 7 years |
| Website analytics | 26 months |

## 7. Data Breach Response

1. **Detection:** Identify and contain the breach immediately.
2. **Notification:** Report to the DPO within **1 hour**. DPO reports to authorities within **72 hours** if required.
3. **Assessment:** Determine scope, affected data, and risk level.
4. **Remediation:** Implement fixes to prevent recurrence.
5. **Documentation:** Record the incident, response, and lessons learned.
