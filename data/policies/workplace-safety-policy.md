# Workplace Safety Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy Owner:** Facilities and Operations Department
**Last Updated:** March 5, 2026
**Applies To:** All employees, contractors, and visitors at Northstar Foods facilities

## 1. Purpose

Northstar Foods is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. This policy outlines safety responsibilities, procedures, and emergency protocols for all workplace locations.

## 2. General Safety Rules

- Report all hazards, unsafe conditions, or near-miss incidents to your manager or Facilities immediately.
- Keep work areas clean, organized, and free of obstructions.
- Do not operate equipment without proper training and authorization.
- Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) when required.
- Do not prop open doors that require badge access.

## 3. Emergency Procedures

### 3.1 Fire
- Evacuate immediately via the nearest marked exit when the fire alarm sounds.
- Do not use elevators during evacuation.
- Proceed to the **designated assembly point** (parking lot A for Building 1; courtyard for Building 2).
- Do not re-enter the building until the all-clear is given by emergency personnel.

### 3.2 Medical Emergency
- Call **911** immediately for life-threatening emergencies.
- Notify the front desk or security at extension **5555**.
- AED devices are located in the main lobby of each floor.
- First aid kits are in all break rooms and near restrooms.

### 3.3 Severe Weather
- Move to interior rooms or hallways away from windows.
- Follow instructions from Facilities via Slack **#emergency-alerts** and the PA system.
- Do not leave the building until the all-clear is given.

## 4. Ergonomics

- All employees are entitled to an **ergonomic assessment** of their workstation.
- Request an assessment through the **IT Service Desk** or **Facilities**.
- Northstar Foods provides sit-stand desks, ergonomic chairs, and monitor arms upon request.
- Remote employees may use their **$500 home office stipend** for ergonomic equipment.

## 5. Visitor Policy

- All visitors must check in at the front desk and receive a **visitor badge**.
- Visitors must be accompanied by a Northstar Foods employee at all times.
- Visitor badges must be returned upon departure.
- Report unaccompanied or unbadged individuals to security immediately.

## 6. Incident Reporting

- All incidents, injuries, and near-misses must be reported within **24 hours**.
- Reports are submitted via the **Safety Incident Form** on the company intranet.
- Facilities investigates all reports and implements corrective actions.
- Retaliation for reporting safety concerns is strictly prohibited.

## 7. Training

- **New hires:** Complete workplace safety orientation during the first week.
- **All employees:** Complete annual safety refresher training.
- **Specialized roles:** Additional training as required (e.g., chemical handling, electrical safety).
- Fire drill participation is **mandatory** (drills occur quarterly).
